How to Get There

San Diego International airport (SAN) is the closest airport to Anza Borrego State Park. It is about 1.5 hours driving. We recommend flying in to SAN (there are direct JetBlue, Alaska, and Delta flights from NYC). Then, we recommend renting a car to drive to Anza Borrego. We will coordinate arrival times and car rentals closer to the date, so you can pair up and drive with others.

Camping

AirBnb / Hotels

There are great AirBnb or hotel options either in Julian, CA (~30 minute drive to site, in the mountains, cooler) or Borrego Springs, CA (~35 minute drive to site, in the lower parts of the desert, may be quite hot in April!).
